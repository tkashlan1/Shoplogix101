Adding Downtime Reasons

1. Click on the machine you want to add Downtime Reasons to.

2. Click on "Configure Reasons."

3. Click on "Add Reason."

4. Add the name of the new Downtime Reason and make any other changes as needed then click on the green save button.
Make sure you save your config changes.

Deleting Downtime Reasons

1. Click on the machine you want to delete a reason for.

2. Click on "Configure Reasons."

3. Click on a reason you want to delete.

4. Click on "Delete Reason" then click the green circle to save. Make sure you save your config changes.

Grouping

1. Click on the machine you want to add a Group for.

2. Click on "Configure Reasons."

3. Click on the Downtime Reason you want to place in a group/create a group for then click on "Edit Reason."

4. Type the name of the group in the appropriate box. Make sure you save your config changes.

Reordering Reasons

1. Click on the machine you want to reorder Downtime Reasons for.

2. Click on "Configure Reasons."

3. A reason needs to be in a group to edit the order inwhich it shows up on the whiteboard. An exmaple of a reorder
is: "3,2|Operator" where "3" is the order of the group, "2" is the order of the reason in the group and "Operator" is the name of the group.
Once you reorder the reason, press the green save circle. Note, there is a pipe (|) right before the group name. Make sure you save your config changes.
