Setting up Reports

1. Click on a machine you want to set up a report for.

2. Click on "Reports."

3. Click on the report you want to set up. You can also search for it with the search bar on the top of this pop-up.

4. To save your report as a favourite, refer to the Flash Favourites page.

Editing Timelines

1. Once you open a report and the bar at the bottom on your screen turns from blue to green, click on the small calender icon.

2. Make any adjustments on the pop-up.

3. Click on the green circle to save.

Viewing Saved Reports

1. Hover your mouse on one of the star icons found next to machine names, once you find the report you want to view, click on it.
