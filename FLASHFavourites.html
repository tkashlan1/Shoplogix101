Setting up Favourites

1. Click on the machine you want to create a favourite for.

2. Click on "Favourites."

3. Click on "Favourites."

4. Click "New."

5. Enter a name for the new report. For example: "Press 1 Hourly Reports."

6. Click on the "Other" dropdown menu to select a star colour matching the type of report.

7. From the "Exact Date and Time" dropdown menu, pick an appropriate timeline. Refer to the Shoplogix Wiki for more details on each timeline option.

8. Pick a range depending on what the customer has asked for by clicking the start date from the left side and end date from the right side.

9. Click on the green "Save" button on the top right of this pop-up screen.

10. If this favourite needs to be emailed, click on the favourite then the "Share" button.

11. Type "admin" for both the username and password then click the green "Save" button.

12. Click on "Email."

13. Type the recipients email adresses, set how often the email should be sent and when should the emails begin sending. Click the green "Save" button.

14. Type "admin" for both the username and password then click the green "Save" button.

Editing Emailed Favourites

1. Click on the machine you want edit.

2. Click on "Favourites."

3. Click on "Favourites."

4. Click on the favourite you want to make changes to then on "Email."

5. Make the necessary changes then click on the green "Save" button on the top right of this pop-up screen.

6. Type "admin" for both the username and password then click on the green button to save.
